Login (Token)

How to obtain token:

  • 1) Login on the AMIV Website and select keep me logged in
  • 2) While still on the AMIV Website open the Javascript console:
    • Firefox:Ctrl Shift +K
    • Chrome / Edge:Ctrl Shift + J
  • Enter the following to get your Token:

    JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem("session")).token
